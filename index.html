Bio

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the computer science department in Aalborg university. My research interests include Energy Data Management, Spatial-temporal Data Management, Education Data Management, and Digital Twin Technology. I have published 15+ publications in prestigious journals and conferences and boasts an h-index of 5 and has accumulated more than 160 citations, as indexed by Google Scholar. I have served as a Program Committee Member for prestigious conferences such as AAAI 2024 and DASFAA 2024. Additionally, my expertise has been recognized through his roles as a reviewer for top-tier journals and conferences including TKDE, IJCAI, CIKM, and EMNLP.
