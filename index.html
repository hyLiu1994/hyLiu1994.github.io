About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Data Engineering, Science and Systems (DESS) group at the Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University (AAU), where I joined as a Postdoctoral Researcher in November 2023. My research vision is to advance interpretable and trustworthy AI-driven data management and analysis, with a particular focus on spatio-temporal and mobility-related applications.

I have authored or co-authored over 25 peer-reviewed publications, including six first-author papers, spanning top-tier venues such as PVLDB, ICML, WWW, IEEE TNNLS, ACM TOSEM, Artificial Intelligence Journal, and Frontiers of Computer Science, achieving an h-index of 10 with over 700 citations on Google Scholar.

I actively contribute to the research community as Area Chair for NeurIPS 2026 (Position Track), Program Committee member for AAAI (2025–2026), IJCAI 2026, DASFAA (2024–2026), and BESC (2025–2026), and Guest Editor for special issues in Sustainability and Journal of Imaging. I regularly review for journals and conferences including TKDE, EAAI, EMNLP, CIKM, Information Fusion, and Machine Learning.

Research Interests

My current research interests include:

  • Trustworthy AI for Spatio-temporal Data
  • Agentic AI
